Strategic Digital Architecture and Content Deployment Blueprint for the Coach Tina Global Transformation Brand
The evolution of the digital presence for Tinashe Mujera, professionally recognized as Coach Tina, requires a transition from a service-oriented website to a comprehensive intellectual property ecosystem. As a Master’s degree holder in Commerce and an accredited Neuro-Linguistic Programming (NLP) and Life Coach with over 13 years of leadership experience, Tinashe Mujera occupies a unique market position that bridges corporate strategy and spiritual restoration.1 The objective of this architectural overhaul is to create a premium, high-authority platform that mirrors the psychological depth and personal relatability of global brand leaders like Mel Robbins while maintaining the soulful, faith-grounded authority inherent in the Coach Tina identity.2 This report details the structural, visual, and content-driven strategies necessary to establish a digital "home" that facilitates deep individual transformation, corporate cultural shifts, and the long-term stewardship of the next generation through the Empower U Academy.1
The Robbins-Inspired Authority Framework: Positioning and Persona
The success of the Mel Robbins digital model rests upon the "Three Legs of the Creator Stool": a definitive book, a global show, and high-impact public speaking.4 For Coach Tina, this model must be adapted to integrate her existing body of work, including her three transformational books, the Faith in Action podcast, and her extensive speaking history on national platforms like SABC 2 and Radio Pulpit.1 To achieve a "premium" feel, the website must transition the user from viewing Tinashe Mujera as a person to viewing her as an institution—a comprehensive resource library for human growth.2

Implementation Mechanics: Frictionless Conversion and Systems
The transition from a "visitor" to a "client" must be frictionless, leveraging automation to manage the administrative burden of a multi-offer business.6
Booking Automation via Calendly
The primary "Book a Call" CTA should lead to a streamlined Calendly interface, replacing traditional contact forms that often lead to data loss or delayed responses.5
Strategic Pre-Screening: The booking flow should include 3-5 qualifying questions (e.g., "What is the primary challenge keeping you from your 'Rise' phase?") to ensure the discovery call is focused and effective.25
Automated Nurture Sequence: After a booking is confirmed, the system should automatically send a welcome email containing a free "Leadership Reset" guide or a link to a specific "Faith in Action" episode to build anticipation for the session.17
